CooKnight [WIP]

A Cook Knight after his stolen cook recipes!

Cooknight will be one of the main games I'll work on during college. The game is a silly cartoonish "automatic runner", where you have to focus on precise jumps and some attacks to help you transverse the different stages.

The Goal is to reach the end of each stage, and eventually get back your cook book stolen by the Ghosts!!

Project: Audiogame [WIP]

A project to include vision deficient people to games

Videogames are a huge media of entertaining, however not everyone can enjoy them, including blind people.

This minor project is an attempt to create a game to include blind people to have play games as well. By using sounds into the game design to provide guidance and depth into play!
